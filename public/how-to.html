How to Use
the Daily Ops Report

Your two-minute daily habit — capture the day, spot what needs attention, and keep your site’s streak alive. Here’s everything you need.

Quick Start

Three steps, every day

The whole thing takes about two minutes. Do it once a day, ideally before you head out.

🔑
1

Log in

Open bbdor.netlify.app and sign in with your email. You’ll land on today’s report, already set to your site.

✍️
2

Fill it out

Work down the sections. Enter the numbers and add a quick note wherever the count is more than zero. It saves as you type.

🐤
3

Submit

Hit Submit report before 11:59 PM. You’ll get a little celebration — and your streak grows.

Filling It Out

What goes in each section

A number on its own tells half the story — the notes are where the value is. Whenever a count is above zero, add the names, rooms, or reasons.

👶

Attendance

Pre-school + subsidy counts. The total adds up automatically.

⏱️

Labor

Total hours, overtime (add names), and director minutes in rooms.

📣

Enrollment / Marketing

Tours, inquiries, reg fees, new starts, enrollments, terminations.

🎯 Aim for 15 enrollment comms out each day.

👥

Staff

Call-outs, RTO, sent home, terminations, recruiting time, future hires.

✅

Director Packet

Yes or No. If No, a quick note on what got in the way is required.

📝

Director Report

Free-text notes for the day — follow-ups, write-ups, anything leadership should see. Add as many lines as you need.

💾

It autosaves. Every change is saved as a draft as you type — even if your wifi blips. Come back later and pick up where you left off. There’s only ever one report per site, per day.

🔥 How streaks work

  • ✓ File on a workday (Mon–Fri) and your streak grows. Weekends and holidays don’t count against you.
  • ✓ Missed a day? You get one free “streak freeze” each month — a single sick day won’t reset you.
  • ✓ File the day after a miss and earn the “Back on Track” badge. 💪

For Leadership

The full dashboard (admin)

Admins get the complete real-time picture across all three sites — the thing a copied spreadsheet could never do.

  • ★KPIs at a glance — average attendance, overtime %, enrollment comms vs. goal, net new starts.
  • ★Pipeline & panels — enrollment funnel, attendance by site, staff watch, packet compliance.
  • ★Red flags & celebrations — missed reports, overtime, packet gaps surfaced instantly; wins surfaced too.
  • ★Export — download any week as CSV or JSON.
  • ★Director View — choose exactly which cards the directors see on their Team Dashboard.
⚠️
Red flags aren’t about blame. They flag operational things to look at — never a director’s score. Points and flags are completely separate.
Pro Tips

Make every report a great one

🗒️
Always add notes where a count is above zero — names, rooms, reasons. That’s what makes the data useful.
📣
Log all comms out toward the daily goal of 15 — every two-way touch counts.
🔁
Don’t break the chain. Even a quick, honest report keeps your streak alive.
